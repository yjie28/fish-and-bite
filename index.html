Fishes and Bites
A 4 digit secret number will be generated and your job is to guess the secret number.
The number will be in between 0 to 9 and none of the numbers will be repeated.
If you guessed a correct number in its correct position, you will get 1 fish.
If you guessed a correct number but in its wrong position, you will get 1 bite.
Your goal is to get 4 fishes to win the game.
If you are ready, click "Start Game!" to begin the game!
